Dabeli

Dabeli is a common Chaat dish which is indigenous to both Gujarat and Maharashtra. It is easily available in most of the Chaat stores in Mumbai.

Cholebhature

The famous CholeBhature is a combination of the huge Puri otherwise known as Bhatura and is served with a side of lip-smackingly delicious Choley!

Grilled Sandwich

Aloo Sandwich is a classic but simple dish which requires a stuffing with Potatoes. The Potatoes are stuffed with the bread which is then grilled on a flame to produce the much-desired Grilled Sandwich.

Dosa

Dosa is the most common Indian snack of all time. Owing to its popularity, outlets like Saravana Bhavan have found its way into popularity in the international markets! Dosa has many varieties in India, all of which are loved and appreciated.

The Works Pizza

And when the supreme just isn’t enough, you’re ready for the works. It’s a step up in heartiness from the supreme. The “works” usually involves a wide variety of veggie toppings including onions, olives, and mushrooms, plus bacon and pepperoni.

Chowmein

Chowmein is a Chinese dish. It is very popular in India and other countries too. Its a stir fried noodles. Chowmein contains good amount of protein as it is made with lot of vegetables like capsicum, onion, carrot, cabbage, Soya sauce, vinegar is added to Chowmein to give a tangy taste.

Manchurian

Dry Manchurian is served as a snacks. Manchurian is a fried vegetable balls. For making vegetable balls we can use cauliflower, cabbage, spring onions, French beans, carrot, corn starch can be used for binding all the ingredients.

Veg Schezwan Fried Rice

It is a Chinese variant of Fried rice cooked in a Indian style with schezwan sauce which gives it a spicy chilli garlic flavour.
