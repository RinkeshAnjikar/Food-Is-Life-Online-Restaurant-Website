Sweets And Desserts

Shrikhand

Shrikhand is a traditional Indian sweet rather like an assorted silky-smooth yoghurt. It’s prepared using creamy hung curd, powdered sugar, saffron, and cardamom. Shrikhand is quite popular in Gujarat and Maharashtra.

Ras Malai

Have you ever tried cottage cheese with clotted cream? Well, ras malai is milky soft, sweet Indian cottage cheese balls dunked in clotted cream. India and Bangladesh claim to be the origins of this cold dessert. In Hindi, ras means juice and malai means clotted cream.

Rabri

Chocolate Mousse Cup

A pretty, ruffled chocolate cup filled with a rich, creamy milk chocolate mousse topped with a fresh, sweet strawberry, real chocolate sprinkles and served with a berry coulis. This Chocolate Mousse Cup is definitely a chocolate piece de resistance.
